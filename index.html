About Us

We are a small family business that has been making baskets for generations. We pride ourselves on using high quality materials and traditional techniques to create beautiful and functional baskets.

Kenyan made baskets are true works of art, crafted by skilled artisans who use traditional techniques passed down through generations. These baskets are not only functional but also beautiful and unique, each one tells a story of the culture and tradition of the people who made it.

These baskets are made using locally sourced materials, including sisal, banana leaves, and raffia. The artisans carefully hand-stitch and weave each basket, creating intricate patterns and designs that are truly one-of-a-kind. The baskets are available in a variety of sizes, from small trinket baskets to large shopping baskets that can be used to carry groceries or other items.

In addition to being beautiful and unique, these baskets are also eco-friendly and sustainable. Many of the materials used to make the baskets are renewable and biodegradable, and the traditional techniques used by the artisans are gentle on the environment.

Not only do these baskets serve a useful purpose, they also give a unique touch of elegance and beauty, by owning one of these baskets you are directly connecting with the culture and people of Kenya, and make a difference in the lives of local artisans, promoting a culture of sustainability and eco-friendliness.
